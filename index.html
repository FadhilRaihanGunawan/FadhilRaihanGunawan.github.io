Visit to NALI 2023

REFLECTION: We interviewed participants that were part of the NALI 2023. We were able to find out many new inventions that can potentially create a better IOT world. From educations to cleaning the ecosystem, It helped me realize how amazing humanity is when it comes to creating the next-level technology. The poster that I interviewed was about the development of E-bike Tracking system for Bike sharing in Promoting Transport Resilience. The E-bike Monitoring System at UTM is a technology service to locate and also take care of the E-bikes. It aids students to quickly situate close-by e-bikes making use of a mobile application, conserving them from looking around. The system additionally allows UTM monitor e-bike places avoiding burglary. The advantages consist of effective e-bike monitoring plus being ecologically friendly. No drawbacks are discussed. Future strategies include obtaining students to maintain e-bikes in enabled locations. Considering that it's still in advancement, There is no individual responses. The representation highlights the favorable effect on school life as well as the atmosphere, recommending it can influence even more advancements. On the whole, The E-bike Tracking System is attended to be a reliable device for a greener as well as far better school.
